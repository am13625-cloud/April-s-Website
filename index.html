About April

April is an indie-pop artist that blends ethereal vocals, nostalgic melodies, and atmospheric production. Her songs document late-night moments, unspoken feelings, and coming-of-age experiences. She combines the intimacy of bedroom-pop with cinematic indie textures.

For fans of Billie Eilish, Clairo, and Maggie Rogers.
